About Me

Modupeola Fagbenro is a dedicated and results-driven Master of Science in Data Science student at George Washington University. With strong expertise in analytics and machine learning, particularly in Natural Language Processing (NLP), she has worked on impactful projects as a Data Scientist and Business Intelligence Analyst/Data Analyst. Her commitment to volunteerism is evident through her active contributions to sustainability data science research at George Washington University's Data Science for Sustainability Development Hub. Modupeola has interned at Amazon.com LLC as a Business Intelligence Engineer, where she focused on creating data models, using Large Language Model (LLM) to optimize long-running queries on the cluster. She is also engaged in research at George Washington-Data Science for Sustainability Development Hub. In addition to her professional and academic pursuits, Modupeola volunteers as a marketing team member for the George Hackathon at George Washington University and with Women in Data Science Puget Sound in Washington, showcasing her dedication to community and collaboration.
