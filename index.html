About Me

Modupeola Fagbenro is Data Scientist, Business Intel Engineer, ML Enthusiast. A Graduate of Data Science at George Washington University (May 2025). With growing expertise in analytics, machine learning, and data processing, she is developing specializations in Natural Language Processing (NLP), Time Series Analysis, and Multi-Agent Systems. Her professional experience includes a Business Intelligence Engineering internship at Amazon.com LLC, where she engineered a robust enterprise-wide pipeline monitoring system, enhancing SLA to 99.5%. She also innovatively developed an LLM-powered query optimization chatbot, reducing execution time by 45%, demonstrating her growing capabilities in applied machine learning. Modupeola's academic projects showcase her technical interests, including her work on a Heart Attack Risk Prediction system using ensemble models and her AI Medical Research Assistant that leveraged RAG systems with LLMs. Her graduate research focuses on Multi-Agent Systems that integrate knowledge graphs and large language models to analyze complex data interactions. At George Washington University's Data Science for Sustainability Development research initiative, she uses Python and machine learning algorithms to analyze sentiment in social media posts during crisis events and implements topic modeling with scikit-learn. She actively collaborates with cross-functional teams to translate research findings into actionable recommendations. As an ML enthusiast with experience in both academic research and business applications, Modupeola combines analytical rigor with a passion for solving real-world problems through data science. Her commitment to continuous learning and practical implementation of ML concepts drives her academic and professional development.
